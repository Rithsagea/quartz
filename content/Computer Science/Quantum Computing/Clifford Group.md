The Pauli group $P=\langle\sigma_X,\sigma_Y,\sigma_Z\rangle$ is generated with the following matrices.
$$
I=\bmatrix{1&0\\0&1}\qquad
\sigma_X=\bmatrix{0&1\\1&0}\qquad
\sigma_Y=\bmatrix{0&-i\\i&0}\qquad
\sigma_Z=\bmatrix{1&0\\0&-1}
$$

The $n$-qubit Pauli group is then given with the $n$-fold tensor product over $\mathbb C$, $P_n=\bigotimes_{i=1}^nP$.
